Frequently Asked Questions - Online Shopping Apps

What is Amazon's return policy?

Amazon allows returns within 30 days of receipt for most items. Some products have different return policies, so be sure to check the product details before making a purchase.

How do I track my order on Amazon?

You can track your Amazon order by visiting the "Your Orders" section in your account. You will also receive an email with tracking details once your order has shipped.

What payment methods does Amazon accept?
  • Credit Cards
  • Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Amazon Pay
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
How do I return a product on Flipkart?

To return a product on Flipkart, go to "My Orders," select the item you wish to return, and follow the instructions. Returns are allowed within 10 days of delivery for most items.

What is Flipkart's delivery time?

Flipkart typically delivers products within 3-7 business days, depending on the location and the type of product ordered. Some products may be delivered faster with Flipkart's Express delivery service.

Can I apply discount coupons on Flipkart?

Yes, Flipkart allows you to apply discount coupons during checkout. You can find active coupons on the Flipkart website or app.

How do I use Myntra's sale offers?

Myntra offers various sale events like "End of Season Sale," "Myntra Fashion Weekend," and more. To avail of discounts, you can either check the sale section or apply specific promo codes during checkout.

What is Myntra's return policy?

Myntra offers a 30-day return policy for most products, provided the items are unused and in their original packaging. The return policy for certain products like innerwear and cosmetics may vary.

Can I cancel an order on Myntra?

You can cancel an order on Myntra before it is dispatched. If the order has already been shipped, you can initiate a return after receiving the product.

How do I track my Myntra order?

To track your Myntra order, visit "My Orders" in your account. You will be able to see the tracking details for shipped products.
